Data 2 Grand Slams

Do you know what are Grand Slams?


The Grand Slam tournaments（大满贯）, also called majors, are the four most important annual tennis events.
They offer the most ranking points, prize money, public and media attention, the greatest strength and size of field.

- Australian Open in mid January
- French Open around late May
- Wimbledon in June-July
- U.S. Open in August-September
